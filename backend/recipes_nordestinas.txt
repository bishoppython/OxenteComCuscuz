# Northeastern Brazilian Recipes Dataset
# Nutritional information and recipes for RAG system

## Acarajé
- Ingredients: Black-eyed peas, onion, dendê oil, salt, pepper, shrimp, caruru
- Nutritional info: High protein (from shrimp and beans), rich in healthy fats (dendê oil), moderate calories
- Preparation: Soak black-eyed peas for 24h, blend with onion and spices. Fry in dendê oil. Serve with shrimp and caruru.
- Nutrition notes: Good for muscle building due to high protein content from shrimp and beans

## Baião de Dois
- Ingredients: Rice, beans (fradinho), queijo coalho, bacon, bacon grease, butter, onion, coriander
- Nutritional info: Balanced protein (beans and cheese), good carbs (rice), healthy fats (cheese and bacon)
- Preparation: Fry bacon and onions, add rice and beans. Season with butter and cheese. Add coriander.
- Nutrition notes: Excellent for weight gain or muscle building due to protein and complex carbs

## Carne de Sol com Macaxeira
- Ingredients: Sun-dried meat, cassava, butter, onion, garlic, tomato, coriander, alcatrão sauce
- Nutritional info: High protein meat, complex carbs (cassava), essential vitamins and minerals
- Preparation: Soak meat overnight, then cook with cassava and vegetables. Season with alcatrão.
- Nutrition notes: Ideal for muscle building due to high protein content from sun-dried meat

## Tapioca
- Ingredients: Tapioca flour, filling options (queijo coalho, coconut, eggs)
- Nutritional info: Gluten-free carbs, good energy source, low protein unless filled with cheese
- Preparation: Heat tapioca flour in special pan to form crepe. Add fillings.
- Nutrition notes: Good pre-workout food due to easily digestible carbs

## Bode Guisado (Goat Stew)
- Ingredients: Goat meat, onion, garlic, tomato, coriander, ginger, oil, salt, pepper
- Nutritional info: Lean protein, low in saturated fat compared to beef, iron-rich
- Preparation: Marinate goat meat, then stew with vegetables and spices.
- Nutrition notes: Excellent for muscle gain and recovery, rich in protein and minerals

## Peixe na Brasa (Grilled Fish)
- Ingredients: Fresh fish, dendê oil, salt, pepper, onion, coriander, lime
- Nutritional info: High-quality protein, omega-3 fatty acids, low calories
- Preparation: Marinate fish with spices and grill over charcoal.
- Nutrition notes: Perfect for weight loss and lean muscle maintenance

## Caldo de Sururu
- Ingredients: Sururu (mussels), water, onion, garlic, tomato, cilantro, malagueta pepper
- Nutritional info: High protein from shellfish, low calories, rich in minerals
- Preparation: Cook mussels in seasoned water with vegetables until shells open.
- Nutrition notes: Good for lean protein intake, low calorie meal option

## Munguzá
- Ingredients: Corn, sugar, coconut milk, condensed milk, cinnamon
- Nutritional info: Complex carbs from corn, healthy fats from coconut, moderate calories
- Preparation: Cook corn with coconut milk and sweeteners. Add cinnamon.
- Nutrition notes: Good post-workout meal due to carbs and healthy fats

## Rabada
- Ingredients: Tail of ox, onion, garlic, tomato, coriander, vinagre, oil
- Nutritional info: High protein and collagen, good for joint health
- Preparation: Slow-cook ox tail with vegetables until tender.
- Nutrition notes: Excellent for recovery due to collagen content

## Camarão na Moranga (Shrimp in Pumpkin)
- Ingredients: Shrimp, pumpkin, coconut milk, crayfish paste, spices
- Nutritional info: High protein shrimp, fiber from pumpkin, healthy fats
- Preparation: Prepare pumpkin as container, fill with seasoned shrimp mixture and bake.
- Nutrition notes: Low-calorie, high-protein meal for weight management
